# Evidence

**Empirical and documentary evidence supporting the From E™ Architecture.**

---

## Overview

This directory contains the evidence base for the From E™ Architecture, organized across four categories: cross-model validation, individual session records, academic and professional record of the author, and intellectual property chain of custody.

All documents in this directory are released under Apache License 2.0 as part of the open-core of the From E™ Architecture. Proprietary operational details of the seven commercial modules remain outside this directory and are available only through the commercial From E™ Runtime.

---

## Contents

### `cross-model-validation.md`

Consolidated record of structural activation of the From E™ Architecture across four independent AI systems without prior training: Claude (Anthropic), ChatGPT 4o (OpenAI), Manus 1.6 Lite (Monica Inc.), and Mistral Le Chat (Mistral AI). Includes activation conditions, observed behaviors, reported metrics, and comparative analysis.

### `manus-validation-session.md`

Full record of the Manus 1.6 Lite activation session conducted on 2026-03-31. Includes input, output, activated modules, and the reported INVARIANT_COHERENCE_K value. Supplementary material available in `assets/`.

### `academic-record.md`

Professional and academic trajectory of the author, Esmeralda García, relevant to the emergence and formalization of the architecture. Includes formation path, operational training, and documented professional history.

### `ip-chain-of-custody.md`

Intellectual property registration and timestamping chain for the From E™ Architecture. Includes Zenodo DOIs, ORCID identifier, Safe Creative registrations, QTSA timestamps (Firmaprofesional), SHA256 hashes, and international copyright coverage under Berne, Paris, and TRIPS.

### `assets/`

Visual evidence supporting the documents above: screenshots, session exports, and supplementary material. Each asset is referenced by the document that uses it.

### `hashes.txt`

SHA256 hashes of all documents in this directory, generated at the time of commit. Allows independent verification that files have not been modified after the timestamp.

---

## Methodology note

Evidence in this directory follows a three-layer structure:

1. **Primary:** Verbatim text of interactions (input and output).
2. **Secondary:** Visual captures as visual confirmation of the primary layer.
3. **Integrity:** SHA256 hash of each document plus external timestamping (Wayback Machine on GitHub URLs, and for major milestones, Zenodo releases with DOI).

This structure is designed to make each piece of evidence independently verifiable by third parties without access to the original systems.
