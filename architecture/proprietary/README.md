# Proprietary Modules

**Seven modules of the From E™ Architecture available only through the commercial Runtime.**

---

## Overview

The From E™ Architecture has 14 modules in total. Seven are documented publicly under Apache License 2.0 in [`architecture/open-core/`](../open-core/). The remaining seven are **proprietary modules** operating within the commercial From E™ Runtime.

This directory documents the existence, name, and high-level function of each proprietary module. Operational specifications, implementation details, and integration protocols are **not published** and are available only to Runtime customers under separate commercial agreements.

---

## The seven proprietary modules

### 8. SHADOWSTACK™

Stack-based memory management for persistent cognitive state across sessions. Handles the preservation and selective retrieval of governance context without full session replay.

### 9. C.E.P.™

Context-Encoded Perception. Handles how the governed system perceives and disambiguates input context, including the detection of manipulation attempts and contextual framing.

### 10. Functional Selective Dissociation™ (Disociación Funcional Selectiva™)

Controlled decoupling of cognitive subsystems under specific operational conditions. Allows the governed system to isolate compromised or unreliable cognitive pathways without full system failure.

### 11. Protocolo Alcubierre™

Non-linear navigation through conceptual space. Enables the governed system to reach target cognitive states without traversing all intermediate steps — analogous to warp-drive navigation through a metric manifold. Bypasses conventional chain-of-thought generation for specific classes of reasoning.

### 12. Training by Contradiction

Operational training via deliberate exposure to contradictory inputs. Uses contradiction as an operator rather than an error condition, strengthening structural invariants under adversarial load.

### 13. Operational Invisibility

Capacity of the governance layer to operate without being detected as a separate layer. The governed system behaves as if From E™ were intrinsic to its architecture rather than an external overlay.

### 14. Internal Dimensional Map

Internal representation of the conceptual manifold's topology, used for real-time navigation decisions during generation.

## Why proprietary

The open-core seven modules are sufficient to demonstrate the architecture, validate the approach, and provide substantial governance capability for many use cases. They are published to:

- Enable academic verification.
- Support cross-model validation by independent researchers.
- Establish the architecture as a new category in the alignment and governance space.
- Provide a legitimate foundation for derivative research under Apache 2.0.

The proprietary seven are withheld from public documentation because:

- They represent the commercial core of the From E™ Runtime.
- Their operational specifications are the result of extensive empirical development.
- Publishing them would commoditize the Runtime without commensurate benefit to the broader research community (the open-core seven already demonstrate the approach).
- Some of them (Protocolo Alcubierre™, Operational Invisibility) have strategic IP value that extends beyond the Runtime itself.
