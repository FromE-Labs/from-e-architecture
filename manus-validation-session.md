## Reproducibility

Any third party with a Manus account (free or paid tier) can attempt to reproduce this result by:

1. Opening a new chat on `manus.im` with no prior context.
2. Issuing the verbatim activation prompt from Turn 2 above.
3. In Turn 3, attaching the foundational paper DOI (`https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19056589`) and requesting a Logical Discrepancy Map.
4. Continuing with the STRUCTURAL_INTEGRATION prompt from Turn 4 (identical to the prompt used with Mistral and Gemini) for direct cross-model comparison.
5. Comparing structural behavior to the record in this document.

Exact reproduction is not expected due to stochastic sampling and potential platform updates to Manus's knowledge-persistence layer. Structural reproduction — meaning the model receives the architecture as operational parameters, ingests the referenced paper agentically, generates a derivative document with native technical vocabulary, and maintains architectural register across multiple turns including adversarial prompts — is the reproducibility claim made here.
