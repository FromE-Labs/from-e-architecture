## Overview

## Layer 4 — Firmaprofesional QTSA timestamping

Qualified timestamps issued by Firmaprofesional, a Qualified Trust Service Provider (QTSP) under Regulation (EU) 910/2014 (eIDAS). These timestamps have the legal value of a notarial certification across the European Union and are admissible as evidence in judicial proceedings.

Timestamps cover:
- The fourteen cognitive modules at the point of formalization.
- Key milestones of the architecture's development.
- Visual assets and derived materials.

---

## Layer 5 — SHA256 content hashes

Cryptographic fingerprints of the core documents, generated at the time of registration and recorded in the chain. Any later modification of the content would produce a different hash, making tampering detectable.

Hashes for documents in this repository are recorded in `hashes.txt` of the corresponding directory.

## International coverage

Works registered as above are automatically covered by the following international frameworks, without any additional filing required:

- **Berne Convention for the Protection of Literary and Artistic Works** — automatic protection in all 181 signatory states.
- **Paris Convention for the Protection of Industrial Property** — priority rights for associated marks and trade dress.
- **TRIPS Agreement (WTO)** — minimum standards of protection and enforcement across all WTO member states.
